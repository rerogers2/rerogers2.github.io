Intro

I have always had a passion for challenges and solving problems. I have been teaching mathematics for 16 years while also dabbling in programming. I finally took the leap to formally study software engineering and have no regrets. Check out my awesome work.

TrueCoders has given me the opportunity to study full stack engineering with C#. My skills have improved dramatically and I have enjoyed every challenge. I hope to expand my skills with multiple languages while developing expertise in technical abilities.

Work

CRUD Database Website

  • Github Repository
    • Video of Application

    This started as a personal project and turned into a CRUD application for my baseball card collection. I implement multiple searches to find cards, insert and delete cards into the collection, and edit the total amount of a card as needed. The search provides two totals; a total number of cards owned and total number of unique cards of that type. I used CMV design to create the web application with C# and CSHTML. The baseball cards are stored in MySQL with SQL commands to sort and search.

    CSV Parsing and Geolocation

  • Github Repository

    • This application takes a list of Taco Bell locations with coordinate points and finds the two furthest locations. The distance uses a Geolocation to account for the curvature of earth over the distances.

    KanyeREST

  • Github Repository

    • An application that simulates a conversation between Kanye West and Ron Swanson through API calls. This application uses the Newtonsoft.JSON NuGet package to call the Kanye URL and parse the data. It also makes a call to the Swanson API and parses the JSON array in order to continue the conversation.

    About

    I am Robert Rogers. Robert is a 16 year veteran in education. He has been inspiring and impacting the future of students through advanced mathematics and statistics. Creating and solving problems has been a passion.

    As a lifetime learner, Robert is always eager to learn new topics, strategies, and techniques to improve the quality of his work. He enjoys implementing quality professional development and teaching what he has learned. He has taken the drive to improve the mathematics departments of two high schools by increasing state scores and mentoring students to pursue high education. Robert has been awarded Teacher of the Year for the 2021-2022 school year.

    Robert is seeking larger challenges and problems to solve. He wants to take his experience and skills and apply them in a way to improve the quality of life for others.
